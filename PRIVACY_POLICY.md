# Afrikoni Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 18, 2026  
**Effective Date:** February 18, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Afrikoni ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our B2B trade platform.

**By using Afrikoni, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.**

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** Name, email, phone number, company name, job title
- **Business Information:** Company registration details, tax ID, business address
- **Verification Documents:** KYC/KYB documents (ID cards, business licenses, certificates)
- **Transaction Data:** RFQs, quotes, orders, payment information, shipping details
- **Communications:** Messages, support tickets, feedback

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, features used, time spent, click patterns
- **Device Information:** IP address, browser type, operating system, device ID
- **Location Data:** Approximate location based on IP address
- **Cookies and Tracking:** Session cookies, analytics cookies, preference cookies

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties
- **Payment Processors:** Transaction status, payment method details
- **Verification Services:** KYC/KYB verification results
- **Logistics Partners:** Shipment tracking data
- **Social Media:** If you connect social accounts (optional)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- **Provide Services:** Facilitate trades, process transactions, manage accounts
- **Verify Users:** Conduct KYC/KYB checks, prevent fraud
- **Communicate:** Send transactional emails, notifications, support responses
- **Improve Platform:** Analyze usage, develop features, fix bugs
- **Marketing:** Send promotional emails (with your consent, opt-out available)
- **Compliance:** Meet legal obligations, enforce Terms of Service
- **Security:** Detect fraud, prevent unauthorized access, protect users

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your data based on:
- **Contract Performance:** Necessary to provide the Service
- **Legitimate Interests:** Fraud prevention, platform improvement, security
- **Legal Obligation:** Compliance with laws (AML, tax, data retention)
- **Consent:** Marketing communications, optional features

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 With Other Users
- Your company profile, product listings, and public information are visible to other users
- Transaction details are shared with counterparties (buyers/sellers)

### 5.2 With Service Providers
We share data with trusted third parties who assist us:
- **Payment Processors:** Stripe, Flutterwave (for payment processing)
- **Cloud Hosting:** Supabase, Vercel (for platform infrastructure)
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics, PostHog (for usage analysis)
- **Email Services:** For transactional and marketing emails
- **Verification Services:** For KYC/KYB checks

### 5.3 For Legal Reasons
We may disclose your information if required by law or to:
- Comply with legal processes (subpoenas, court orders)
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Protect our rights, property, or safety
- Prevent fraud or illegal activity

### 5.4 Business Transfers
If Afrikoni is acquired or merged, your data may be transferred to the new entity.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as:
- Your account is active
- Necessary to provide services
- Required by law (e.g., tax records: 7 years)
- Needed for dispute resolution

After account deletion, we may retain anonymized data for analytics.

## 7. Your Data Rights

### 7.1 Access and Portability
- You can access your personal data through your account settings
- You can request a copy of your data in a portable format

### 7.2 Correction
- You can update your information in account settings
- Contact us to correct inaccurate data

### 7.3 Deletion
- You can request account deletion (subject to legal retention requirements)
- Some data may be retained for compliance or dispute resolution

### 7.4 Objection and Restriction
- You can object to processing for marketing purposes (opt-out)
- You can request restriction of processing in certain circumstances

### 7.5 Withdraw Consent
- You can withdraw consent for marketing emails at any time
- Withdrawal does not affect lawfulness of prior processing

### 7.6 Complaint
- You have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

**To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@afrikoni.com**

## 8. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data:
- **Encryption:** Data in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access, authentication
- **Monitoring:** Security logging, intrusion detection
- **Audits:** Regular security assessments

**However, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.**

## 9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your jurisdiction. We ensure adequate safeguards through:
- **Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)** for EEA data transfers
- **Adequacy Decisions** where applicable
- **Consent** for transfers where required

## 10. Cookies and Tracking

### 10.1 Types of Cookies
- **Essential Cookies:** Required for platform functionality (authentication, security)
- **Analytics Cookies:** Track usage patterns (Google Analytics, PostHog)
- **Preference Cookies:** Remember your settings (language, theme)

### 10.2 Cookie Management
- You can control cookies through browser settings
- Disabling essential cookies may impair platform functionality
- You can opt out of analytics cookies through our cookie banner

## 11. Children's Privacy

Afrikoni is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we discover we have collected data from a child, we will delete it promptly.

## 12. Third-Party Links

### 13.2 Opt-Out
