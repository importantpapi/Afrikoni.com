## THE PROBLEM

### African Trade is Broken

**The numbers:**
- African intra-continental trade is only **15%** of total African trade (vs 60% in Europe, 40% in Asia)
- **$50+ billion lost annually** to trade friction
- **73%** of businesses cite "supplier trust" as #1 barrier
- **42 different currencies** across the continent
- **7-21 days** average customs clearance
- **30%+** of shipments delayed by documentation errors

### Why This Happens

| Problem | Impact |
|---------|--------|
| **Trust Deficit** | No way to verify if a supplier is legitimate |
| **Payment Risk** | No escrow protection, 6-15% wire transfer fees |
| **Logistics Chaos** | No visibility, no tracking, customs nightmares |
| **Information Asymmetry** | Can't verify quality, capacity, or fair pricing |

### The Gap

While Stripe provides payment infrastructure for the internet, Flexport provides logistics infrastructure for global trade, and Plaid provides data infrastructure for fintechâ€”**no platform provides trade infrastructure for Africa**.

This isn't a marketplace problem. It's an **infrastructure problem**.
