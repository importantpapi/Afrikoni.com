## THE PROBLEM WE SOLVE

### African Trade is Broken

Africa's intra-continental trade represents only **15% of total African trade** - compared to 60% in Europe and 40% in Asia. This isn't because African businesses don't want to trade with each other. It's because they **can't trust each other**.

**The barriers:**
- No centralized platform for verified supplier discovery
- Payment fraud and non-delivery risks
- Complex customs and logistics across 54 countries
- 42 different currencies with expensive conversion
- No dispute resolution mechanisms

**The cost:**
- African businesses lose **$50+ billion annually** to trade friction
- Small businesses are locked out of cross-border opportunities
- Economic growth is stunted by fragmentation

### Our Solution

Afrikoni creates the **trust infrastructure** that enables seamless African B2B trade:

1. **Verified Suppliers** - KYC/KYB verification for every business
2. **Protected Payments** - Escrow holds funds until delivery is confirmed
3. **Integrated Logistics** - Track shipments and customs in real-time
4. **Dispute Resolution** - Fair arbitration when issues arise
5. **One Platform** - 54 countries, one marketplace
